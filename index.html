Regime Comparison
Key Notes for AY 2026-27:
• New Regime (Budget 2025): Basic exemption ₹4L. Sec 87A rebate up to ₹60,000 → zero tax for income ≤ ₹12L. Salaried get ₹75,000 std deduction (effective tax-free up to ₹12.75L). Surcharge capped at 25%.
• Old Regime: Unchanged slabs. Sec 87A rebate ₹12,500 for income ≤ ₹5L. Std deduction ₹50,000. All deductions (80C, HRA, etc.) available.
• STCG (Sec 111A) @ 20% | LTCG (Sec 112A) @ 12.5% above ₹1.25L | LTCG Other (Sec 112) @ 20% — these are not eligible for 87A rebate.
• Health & Education Cess @ 4% on tax + surcharge.
• This is an estimate. Consult a Chartered Accountant for official filing.
